Sweeping

EZ Sweep utilizes a large, mechanical broom sweeper with gutter brooms on either side to efficiently clean the curb and the gutter. A rear broom pulls materials into an enclosed hopper. Water is used for dust suppression. These machines are great for heavy sand debris in new developments, road/highway sweeping, milling cleanup and final cleanup on parking lots prior to turning over to the owner.

Milling Cleanup

EZ Sweep saw a need for a milling cleanup service in 2004. This service allows the paving companies to concentrate on what they do best...PAVE. Our lean and mean crews come equipped with a bobcat, bucket and planer which can reach into a tri-axle dump truck and John Deere tractors with front mounted broom attachments AND professional personnel to operate all that equipment!!!

The next time you book a milling machine for that big job, remember to call EZ Sweep to detail those curbs, manholes and tie-in joints.
